BEIJING DESIGN WEEK (BJDW) opened at 17 LANGFANGTOUTIAO Alley, Xicheng Dt., Beijing on 27th, September, 2016. Mr. Feng Zhifeng invited his long-time friend, Mr. Feng Hei (hereinafter referred to as "Feng and Hei") to participate in the exhibition, sharing their thoughts on life and reality from "I PREFER THIS COLORFUL WORLD" to "SHIJIAN". This is the perfect fusion of Art and Furniture.
The Theme of BJDW 2016 is "OPEN HEART DESIGN" by renowned curator Duan Yanling, which is divided into three chapters: MYSPACE, WESPACE, MINDSCAPCE. "MYSPACE" expresses an immersive spatial experience, which through the imagination, meditation, reading and other spiritual activities to achieve our physical and mental nourishment. The two original artworks of "AUGMENTED REALITY" is located in No.4 MYSPACE.

About BEIJING DESIGN WEEK
Beijing Design Week (BJDW) is an annual citywide event co-hosted by National Ministries and the Municipal Government of Beijing during the National Day holiday every year in Beijing. It is a A-class international design event, the largest and most influential platform in Asia for display, promotion, exchange and trading of creative designs.
The BJDW was first held in 2009. Since the beginning of the first fair in 2015 has so far held 6 fairs. This is a week-long design carnival, which has presented in the form of the National Pavilion, inviting many well-known domestic and foreign design brands, designers, design institutions and organizations to participate in the exhibition, the works crosses various fields of eat, wear, use, live and travel.
Attracting over 10 Countries from China and Hong Kong, the Netherlands, Germany, Italy, Austria, Australia, the United States, Iceland and other countries of the designers, design institutes and institutions. Many theme forums, seminars, seminars, interactive activities were launched over the same period. More than 30 domestic and foreign exhibitors of the exhibition and installation covers the construction, furniture, industrial and interior design, graphic design, new media and fashion design and other fields.
